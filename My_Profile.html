My Project!

Overview

The project Is a smart phone app that allows new homeowners or house flippers to take photos of portions of the home that may need rebuilding/remodelling, the app will then give an estimate quote on the cost of renovation and recommend businesses that can complete the job. The app works to streamline the experience of renovating a home and allowing it to become less intimidating to new homeowners and more efficient for house flippers.

Motivation

This project will be especially useful for new homeowners as according to ABS housing data, there were approximately 350,000 first home buyers in 2017-18. This will reduce the stress in some aspects of purchasing a home. This app can also assist in telling home buyers what the cost of renovation is prior to purchasing the property and showing areas that are more cost efficient to live in. Creating this into a smart phone as will allow the product to be low on cost and allow for mass distribution thus allowing for a more streamlined solution to the issue.

Source

Description

With users being prompted to sign in as the application is launched, a database of information ranging from past quotes to payment information is saved, this will allow for a more personalised experience for the user as things such as colour of in-app programs may be changed. The application will make a link between the device's inbuilt camera and the application in order for images to be taken of areas in need of renovation. These images are then processed by built in image recognition and machine learning to identify areas in need of change/renovation. The processed images will then be fed into the database and further processed to provide an estimate quote for renovation. In times that the application is nonresponsive, bugs occur, or unique areas of renovation are unrecognised, users are able to input areas in need of renovation manually, this ensures users satisfaction is maximised. In order to generate the most accurate quote to users, industry averages on renovation jobs and the use of comparable properties that have undergone renovation are used in consideration with other factors to produce the most precise quote, as time progresses, the precision and accuracy of the quote will increase, as the incorporation of in-app learning allows for that. The use of a subscription base system allows for particular users who are interested in investment opportunities to have a better understanding of the prices of renovations for distinct suburbs and areas, this will therefore attract more users to the app for its financial gain. Users can also interact with businesses directly through the app which will allow for better communication via a messenger feature, this adds onto the feature of signing in as unique user numbers are generated for every member. These features is only accessible via the subscription service that the application provides. The application also shows areas that are ‘hot right now’ in the real estate market. As the application progresses and with the addition of further development, the applications features and uses will increase as the use of the app is incorporated into the real estate world. Specific partnerships may be made and thus exposing users of the application to discounts in certain fields such as the price for kitchen appliances or the price of tiling per square metre being reduced, this will act as an incentive for new users to join. The product will also price match certain partnered products which will allow for the lowest price possible for the users.
